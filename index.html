About Me


I am a data analyst with experience running large-scale projects for Fortune 500 companies and small businesses. I love to bring my passion for interpreting data to any team or project I am working on to make smarter business decisions, optimize processes, and impact the bottom line.


My employment and educational experience have honed the following skills:

  • • Use of Python, R, SQL, AWS, and D3 for data analysis and visualizations
  • • Qualitative and Quantitative Data Analysis
  • • Translating technical analysis into actionable insights
  • • VBA for automated reporting and custom solutions
  • • Leadership of cross-functional teams to solve problems and promote positive change
