About Me


I am an engineer/analyst with a passion for exploring and understanding data to help teams make more informed decisions. I love to use data to make an impact and to teach others how they can take advantage of the information available to them.


My background is in industrial engineering with a B.S. in Industrial and Management Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and I am now pursuing grad school to further my knowledge of business analytics and data science.
